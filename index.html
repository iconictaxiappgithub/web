FAQ

Driver Account

Q: I can't log into my app. What should I do?

A: Follow these steps:

  1. Reset your password using the app
  2. Check your email for reset instructions
  3. Reset your password
  4. Log back into your app

Q: Do I need to update to the current version of the app?

A: Yes, it's a must. Failure to update to the current version of the app will result in:

  • Displaying wrong real-time information on the app
  • Errors due to bugs in older versions of the app

Always ensure you're using the latest version for the best experience and accurate information.

Sales Enquiries

Q: When do I get billed?

A: Billing occurs at the beginning of each month.

Q: Can I request a refund if I cancel my advert?

A: Unfortunately, we do not offer refunds once your advert has been published on the app. Your advert will remain active for the entire month, even if you decide to cancel it before the month ends. The advert will be removed at the end of the current billing cycle.
